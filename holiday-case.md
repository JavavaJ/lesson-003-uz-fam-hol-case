## Foydali iboralar:
- **yaxshi ko'rmoq** - to like
- **yoqtirmoq** - to enjoy  
- **xohlamoq** - to want
- **...uchun yaxshi** - good for...
- **...uchun yomon** - bad for...
- **issiq/sovuq havo** - hot/cold weather
- **tinch/shovqinli** - quiet/noisy
- **sayohat qilish** - to travel
